# Academic Navy  (custom theme)

A restrained, scholarly theme for technical/research presentations — deep navy
with a warm scholarly gold accent and steel-blue secondary. Aligned to VNIT's
institutional blue. Built for equation-heavy academic decks (pairs with serif
body + MathJax math).

## Color palette
- **Navy** `#10243E` — primary (headers, rules, body emphasis)
- **Navy 2** `#1B3A5B` — header band / panels
- **Steel** `#3E6691` — secondary accent (sub-headings, links)
- **Gold** `#B07D2B` / **Gold-light** `#D8AE5C` — accent rules, highlights, key terms
- **Ink** `#1C2530` — body text
- **Light** `#EEF2F7` — panel / box backgrounds
- **Paper** `#FFFFFF` — slide background
- **Grid** `#C9D3DF` — hairlines, dividers
- **Red** `#9B2D30` — caution / "reject" / hard limits
- **Green** `#2E6B4F` — "accept" / valid

## Typography
- **Headings**: "Palatino Linotype", "Book Antiqua", Georgia, serif (navy, semibold)
- **Body**: Georgia, "Palatino Linotype", serif (ink)
- **Labels / captions / UI**: "Segoe UI", system-ui, sans-serif
- **Math**: MathJax (Computer-Modern-like serif) — harmonizes with the serif body

## Best used for
Research talks, conference presentations (IMECE/ASME), thesis defenses,
dynamics/controls/ML technical decks, anything equation-dense that should read
as authoritative and institutional.
